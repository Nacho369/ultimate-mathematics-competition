FORMAT OF TEST

Both the two(2) rounds of the competition are different events.

The entire test would be time bound (30 minutes). The test shall be compromising of 25 questions and would be of multiple-choice, accessible online only.

NOTE:

In case of tie in marks, person who submitted the quiz first would be decided as winner.

Exam cannot be re-conducted if someone is unable to submit on time.

Fees shall not be refunded under any circumstances.

WHAT WILL THE TEST COVER?

The test would cover the following topics;

  • Indices
  • Standard Form
  • Logarithims
  • Surds
  • Probability
  • Binary Operations
  • Set
  • Number Bases
  • Arithmetic Sequence
  • Geometric Sequence
